# Privacy Policy for Buzz Quiz Extension

**Last Updated:** December 30, 2025

## Overview

Buzz is a browser extension that generates quiz questions from webpage content using AI. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Webpage Content

- When you use Buzz, we extract text content and the title from the webpage you're currently viewing
- This content is sent to our servers and AI providers (Cohere AI or Gemini AI) to generate quiz questions
- We do not permanently store webpage content on our servers

### Local Storage

The following data is stored locally in your browser:

- Quiz history (questions, scores, timestamps)
- AI provider preference (Cohere AI or Gemini AI)
- Gemini API key (if you choose to use Gemini AI)

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information
- Browsing history
- Passwords or payment information
- Location data
- Cookies or tracking data

## How We Use Your Information

### Quiz Generation

- Webpage content is sent to AI providers solely to generate quiz questions
- Content is processed in real-time and not stored permanently

### Local Storage

- Quiz history is stored locally for your convenience to track progress and retake quizzes
- API keys are stored locally and never sent to our servers
- All local data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us

## Data Sharing

### Third-Party AI Providers

- Webpage content is sent to your chosen AI provider (Cohere AI or Gemini AI) for quiz generation
- These providers have their own privacy policies:
  - [Cohere Privacy Policy](https://cohere.com/privacy)
  - [Google Gemini Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### No Data Sales

- We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties
- We do not share your data with advertisers or marketing companies

## Data Security

- API keys are stored locally in your browser's secure storage
- Communications with our servers use HTTPS encryption
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data in transit

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Clear your quiz history at any time through your browser's extension settings
- Remove your API keys by clearing browser storage or uninstalling the extension
- Stop using the extension at any time

## Data Retention

- Quiz history is stored locally until you clear browser data or uninstall the extension
- We do not retain webpage content after quiz generation
- No data is stored on our servers beyond the session needed for quiz generation

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
